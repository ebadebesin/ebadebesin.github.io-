Quick and Simple Bio About Me


My name is Ebunoluwa Blessing Adebesin, but most times I go by Blessing. I am a junior majoring in Computer science with a minor in Mathematics at Huston Tillotson University, which is the first HBCU in Austin, Texas. I love Computer Science and the broad opportunities it has to offer! I'm not sure what exactly I want to do but I hope to use my degree to bring technology closer to the less priviledge.

My favorite thing about studying Computer Science is the vast opportunities and careers in the field. My goal is definitely to work in a field that allows me to actively inact changes, be it just in bug fixes or designing a website or software, I want to work where I see my work and the effects it has on the company and the customers.
